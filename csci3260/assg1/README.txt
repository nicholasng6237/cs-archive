This is a scene with the Sun, the Earth, a UFO and a spaceship.

Manipulation:
	Escape: Close window
	R: Reset scene
	Space: Play/pause animation
	
	W/S: Rotate the Sun in x-axis
	A/D: Rotate the Sun in y-axis
	Q/E: Rotate the Sun in z-axis

	X/Z: Rotate camera roll
	Comma/Period: Adjust the distance between Sun and Earth
	Minus/Equal: Adjust the size of Earth
